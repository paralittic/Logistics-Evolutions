Fleet Evolutions

Refit/Restock - Meal Time

Details

Overview

This evolution is designed to allow any SES member to conduct a limited refit/restock of a Light or Medium Storm Rescue vehicle during a 1 hour meal time break.

Category Fleet - Refit/Restock
Persons Required 1
Duration 60 Minutes

Process

  1. Remove all rubbish from vehicle
  2. Check fuel level of vehicle, report to driver/team leader if less than 50%
  3. Visual check of tyre pressure
  4. Tarpaulins: Confirm required amount as per AEL/VEL and replenish as necessary
  5. Consumable Rope/String: Replace if less than 25% of drum remaining
  6. Black Plastic: Replace if less than 25% of roll remaining
  7. Chainsaws
    1. Replace any saws tagged for service with fresh saws from store
    2. Check sharpness of chain on each saw and replace chain if blunt
    3. Check fuel churns are above 50% full and refill where necessary
  8. Silicone: Check if minimum 4 tubes are available and caulking gun present and functional
  9. Barrier Tape: Check if minimum of 4cm on any roll is available
